ABOUT R & D

QUEST FOR EXCELLENCE

IEEE-JUIT-SB is organising its first ever virtual R&D expo, a 3-day event including a plethora of events which empowers students to apply their engineering skills to themes of high relevance and global impact. The main attraction of this event would be a 16 Hour Hackathon. To add a splash of creativity ,we have some fun events too such as Meme It Up,Sherlock Holmes and the Virtual Cricket League.There would also be Tech Talks by various speakers having expertise and experience in the field of science and technology. Our goal is to bring all the students and attendees together to give them inspiration, vision and motivation to achieve more and be successful.
