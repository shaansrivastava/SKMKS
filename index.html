MODULO MANAGEMENT

Come to find a CEO in yourself!!
DESCRIPTION
RULES
ROUNDS
DESCRIPTION

Lean in, speak out, have a voice in your organization, and never use the word, “sorry”. Modulo Management is one of the most notable events in R&D which aims to bring out the CEO’s slumbering deep inside each of the participants. As the name itself suggests this event truly consists of aptitude and also the participants should perform a given set of challenging tasks through which participants' management skills and capacities are tested. Hence, this event will surely pave a way for the participants to hone their management skills.

RULES

1. Minimum of one and maximum of 3 members per team is allowed.
2. No participants can be a member of more than one team.
3. Participants can be from any branch, any year and any college.
4. No extra time will be provided under any circumstances for the rounds.
5. Participants involved in any kind of malpractice will be disqualified immediately.
6. Marks will be provided based on the efficiency of the solution.
7. Decisions made by the judge and organizers will be final.

ROUND DETAILS

Round 1:
“APTITUDE PLUS OBSESSION EQUALS GREATNESS”
In this round, the participants(team) will be instructed to solve 20 aptitude questions (*questions will carry extra weightage in case of tie) with duration of 30 minutes through which individual’s ability to perform particular tasks will be analyzed. The top scoring teams will advance to the final round.
Round 2:
“THERE IS NO USE OF TALKING ABOUT THE PROBLEMS UNLESS WE TALK ABOUT THE SOLUTION”
In this round, each team will be given a problem related to an industry/organization which will be sent through a mail. They need to come up with a presentation for solution and have to explain how their ideas can be implemented in real life within the duration of 10 minutes.
